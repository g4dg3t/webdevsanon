WebDevsAnon

Regressive → Progressive

12 december 2016 - Google Launchpad Space, San Francisco

Welcome to WebDevsAnon!

Have you reached your rope's end? Can you no longer face developing another app that pales in comparison to its native brethren? Then come to Web Developers Anonymous, where we'll work together through the 42 stages of progression until we're all making PWAs!
